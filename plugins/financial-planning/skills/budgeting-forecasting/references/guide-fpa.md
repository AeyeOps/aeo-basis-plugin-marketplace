# FP&A (Financial Planning & Analysis) — Expert Resource Guide

## 1. Core Frameworks & Body of Knowledge

- **FPAC Test Specifications** — financialprofessionals.org — Official FP&A competency taxonomy. Part I: planning, budgeting/forecasting, business partnering. Part II: projections, models/analytics, communication. Free PDF.
- **FP&A Board Maturity Model** — fpa-trends.com — 5 maturity levels across 6 dimensions. The industry-standard benchmark for assessing FP&A functions. Free PDF.
- **IMA Statements on Management Accounting** — imanet.org — 50+ authoritative practice guides on management accounting topics
- **Gartner FP&A Research** — gartner.com/en/finance/role/financial-planning-analysis — Coined xP&A. Most influential FP&A tech and organizational research. Some reports free.
- **AFP FP&A Guides** — financialprofessionals.org — Maturity model, building an FP&A function, AI-powered finance. Downloadable PDFs.

## 2. Financial Modeling & Forecasting

### The Essential Free Sources
- **Damodaran (NYU Stern)** — pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar — Full corporate finance + valuation courses (video lectures), downloadable Excel "ginzu" models for DCF, LBO, synergy, capital budgeting. The gold standard.
- **CFI Free Resources** — corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources — 3-statement models, DCF templates, driver-based planning guide, CVP analysis, financial modeling guidelines
- **Wall Street Prep Knowledge Base** — wallstreetprep.com — Rolling forecasts, budget vs actual variance analysis, 3-statement modeling step-by-step
- **Breaking Into Wall Street** — breakingintowallstreet.com/kb — Free video tutorials and knowledge base covering modeling concepts

### Strategic Finance Papers (free PDFs)
- **Mauboussin: Capital Allocation** — morganstanley.com — ROIC analysis, value creation frameworks
- **Mauboussin: Cost of Capital** — Capital allocation and cost of capital interplay
- **Mauboussin: Measuring the Moat** — Competitive advantage assessment

### Scenario & Sensitivity Analysis
- **Monte Carlo Simulation Tutorial** — toptal.com — Applied to FP&A forecasting
- **AFP: Five Scenario Modeling Methods** — financialprofessionals.org
- **The FP&A Guy: Scenario & Sensitivity Analysis** — thefpandaguy.com
