# Expert CPA (Corporate Focus) — Resource Guide

## 1. CPA Exam Structure (2026)

3 Core sections + 1 Discipline (choose one). All within 30 months.

- **FAR** — Financial Accounting & Reporting: US GAAP framework (25-35%), financial statement accounts (30-40%), government/nonprofit
- **AUD** — Auditing & Attestation: audit evidence, professional responsibilities, reporting
- **REG** — Regulation: ethics/Circular 230 (10-20%), business law (10-20%), property transactions, individual/entity tax (28-38%)
- **BAR** — Business Analysis & Reporting (discipline): advanced FASB/SEC, consolidations, leases, revenue
- **TCP** — Tax Compliance & Planning (discipline): deeper entity taxation, tax planning
- **ISC** — Information Systems & Controls (discipline): IT audit, SOC engagements, cybersecurity

### Exam Resources
- **AICPA CPA Exam Blueprints** — aicpa-cima.com — Authoritative document defining every testable topic. Free PDF.
- **AICPA Sample Tests** — One per section with correct answers
- **AICPA Released Questions (via Another71)** — another71.com — 140 officially released questions with explanations
- **Gleim Free CPA Study Materials** — gleim.com — Study guides, practice questions, webinars
- **Becker 2026 FAR/REG Guides** — becker.com — Detailed content breakdowns

## 2. US GAAP Deep Dive

### Primary Source
- **FASB ASC** — asc.fasb.org — Free registration now gives full access (former paywall eliminated). The single authoritative source of US GAAP.

### Big 4 Technical Guides (all free)

**KPMG Handbooks** — kpmg.com/us/en/frv/reference-library/handbooks:
- Income taxes (ASC 740) including Pillar Two
- Business combinations (ASC 805/810)
- Revenue recognition (ASC 606)
- Consolidation (VIEs, voting interest, NCI)
- Derivatives and hedge accounting
- Equity method, foreign currency, EPS, employee benefits, R&D, discontinued ops, ICFR

**Deloitte Roadmap Series** — dart.deloitte.com:
- Revenue (ASC 606), Income Taxes (ASC 740), Business Combinations (ASC 805), CECL (ASC 326), Consolidation (ASC 810), Debt, Carve-Outs, Contracts on Own Equity

**PwC Viewpoint** — viewpoint.pwc.com:
- Income Taxes Guide (ASC 740), Leases Guide (ASC 842) with PDF download, revenue, financial instruments

**EY Financial Reporting Developments** — ey.com/en_us/technical/accountinglink:
- Business Combinations FRD (direct PDF), Credit Impairment ASC 326 (direct PDF), Income Taxes FRD

**RSM Guides** — rsmus.com:
- Revenue (ASC 606), Lease Accounting (ASC 842), Stock Compensation (ASC 718, direct PDF)

**Other**: BDO (ASC 842, CECL), Moss Adams (ASC 842 PDF), Grant Thornton (ASC 606/340-40)

### Key ASC Topics for Corporate Accountants
- ASC 606 — Revenue from contracts with customers (5-step model)
- ASC 718 — Stock-based compensation
- ASC 740 — Income taxes (deferred tax, valuation allowances, uncertain tax positions)
- ASC 805 — Business combinations (acquisition method)
- ASC 810 — Consolidation (VIEs, voting interest model)
- ASC 842 — Leases (operating vs. finance, ROU assets)
- ASC 320/321 — Debt and equity securities
- ASC 326 — CECL model
- ASC 815 — Derivatives and hedging

## 3. Corporate Taxation

### Internal Revenue Code (free full text)
- **Cornell LII: Title 26 (IRC)** — law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26 — Best free interface for reading the IRC
- **Cornell LII: 26 CFR (Treasury Regulations)** — law.cornell.edu/cfr/text/26 — Full text of all Treasury Regs
- **GovInfo: USC Title 26 (PDF)** — Complete IRC as single downloadable PDF

### Key IRC Sections for Corporate Tax
- 11 (corporate rate 21%), 61 (gross income), 162 (business expenses)
- 167/168 (depreciation/MACRS), 179 (expensing), 243 (DRD)
- 267 (related party), 301-318 (distributions), 331-338 (liquidations)
- 351 (tax-free transfers), 368 (reorganizations), 381-384 (carryovers)
- 482 (transfer pricing), 531-537 (accumulated earnings tax)
- 1031 (like-kind exchanges), 1231-1250 (property characterization)
- 1361-1379 (S corporation rules)

### IRS Publications (free, downloadable)
- **Pub 542 — Corporations** — Starting point for C-corp tax compliance
- **Pub 535 — Business Expenses** — Deductibility rules
- **Pub 544 — Sales and Other Dispositions** — Capital gains, 1231, depreciation recapture
- **Pub 550 — Investment Income** — Interest, dividends, capital gains
- **Pub 538 — Accounting Periods and Methods** — Cash vs. accrual, method changes
- **Pub 946 — How to Depreciate Property** — MACRS tables, Section 179, bonus depreciation

### Form 1120
- **Form 1120 Instructions (PDF)** — irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/i1120.pdf — Line-by-line guidance
- **Schedule M-3 Instructions** — irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/i1120sm3.pdf — Book-to-tax reconciliation (>=10M assets)

### IRS Audit Techniques Guides
- **ATG Index** — irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/audit-techniques-guides-atgs — Free PDFs written for IRS examiners. Industry-specific. Reveals exactly what the IRS looks for.
