# Business Financial Audit — Expert Resource Guide

## 1. Authoritative Standards

### Public Company (PCAOB)
- **PCAOB Auditing Standards (full text)** — pcaobus.org/oversight/standards/auditing-standards — Individual AS 1000-3000 series + compiled PDF booklets by fiscal year
- **PCAOB Compiled Standards PDF (post-Dec 2024)** — assets.pcaobus.org/pcaob-dev/docs/default-source/standards/auditing/documents/auditing_standards_audits_after_december_15_2024.pdf — Single-file download
- **AS 2201: Integrated Audit of ICFR** — Cornerstone SOX 404 audit standard
- **AS 2305: Substantive Analytical Procedures** — Using analytical procedures as substantive evidence
- **AS 1215: Audit Documentation** — Working paper standards and retention
- **PCAOB Staff Guidance & Practice Alerts** — pcaobus.org/oversight/standards/staff-guidance — Practical implementation guidance
- **PCAOB Inspection Reports Database** — pcaobus.org/oversight/inspections/firm-inspection-reports — Downloadable CSV/XML/JSON of real audit deficiency findings back to 2018. Learn from actual failures.

### Private Company (AICPA)
- **AU-C Sections (Clarified SASs)** — aicpa-cima.com/resources/download/aicpa-statements-on-auditing-standards-currently-effective — Free 2,072-page PDF of all current auditing standards
- **AICPA Code of Professional Conduct** — pub.aicpa.org/codeofconduct/ethicsresources/et-cod.pdf — Full ethics standards including independence (Section 101)

### Government (GAO)
- **2024 Yellow Book** — gao.gov/assets/d24106786.pdf — Complete Government Auditing Standards PDF. Effective Dec 15, 2025.

### Internal Control
- **COSO 2013 Executive Summary** — sechistorical.org/collection/papers/2010/2013_0501_COSOInternal.pdf — 5-component, 17-principle IC framework
- **KPMG White Paper on COSO 2013** — assets.kpmg.com/content/dam/kpmg/pdf/2016/05/2750-New-COSO-2013-Framework-WHITEPAPER-V4.pdf

### International
- **IAASB Handbook 2023-2024 (Vol 1)** — ifacweb.blob.core.windows.net — Complete ISAs, free PDF

## 2. Open Textbooks

### Internet Archive (free borrow)
- **Arens, Elder, Beasley — Auditing and Assurance Services (14th/16th ed)** — The gold standard US auditing textbook
- **Whittington — Principles of Auditing** — Alternative comprehensive text
- **Hayes et al — Principles of Auditing (ISA-focused)** — Direct PDF download available

### OER
- **OpenStax Principles of Accounting (Vol 1 & 2)** — openstax.org — Free, peer-reviewed. Not audit-specific but essential accounting foundation.
- **LOUIS OER Auditing Collection** — louis.oercommons.org/curated-collections/13 — Curated external financial statement auditing resources

## 3. Big 4 Free Practice Guides

- **KPMG Handbooks** — kpmg.com/us/en/frv/reference-library/handbooks — Dozens of in-depth US GAAP PDFs (financial statement presentation, revenue, consolidation, share-based payments, leases, etc.)
- **Deloitte Roadmap Series** — deloitte.com/us/en/services/audit-assurance/collections/roadmap-series-newsletter — Revenue, business combinations, income taxes, CECL, consolidation, segment reporting
- **PwC Viewpoint (free tier)** — viewpoint.pwc.com — "In depth" series, example accounts, IFRS Talks podcasts
- **EY Atlas Client Edition** — ey.com/en_us/services/audit/atlas-client-edition — Free content channel with downloadable PDFs
- **Deloitte Financial Reporting Checklists** — GAAP disclosure checklists updated quarterly
